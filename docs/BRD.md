## 1. Executive Summary

Soo Solutions Inc supplies and professionally installs security cameras and CCTV systems for commercial and residential properties. This document defines the business requirements for its marketing website — a presenter site whose single job is to turn local search and word-of-mouth traffic into qualified quote requests. The site exists because the company sells a considered, install-and-warranty service that a self-checkout cart cannot represent: prospects need to understand the offer, trust the installer, and reach a person. The primary outcome is **qualified quote requests** (form submissions and tap-to-call), with Google Business Profile actions as the supporting conversion path. Everything on the site is measured against that outcome.

## 3. Target Audience & Personas

Two personas are primary; the third (small-business owner) is a real but secondary segment that overlaps the commercial buyer.

### 3a. Persona — Local Residential Homeowner

| Attribute             | Detail                                                                                                                                                          |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Profile**           | Owns a single-family home in a served town; 35–60; moderate tech comfort; values safety for family and property over gadget features.                           |
| **Goals**             | A professionally installed, reliable camera system they don't have to maintain; clear coverage of entrances, driveway, and yard; remote viewing on their phone. |
| **Pains**             | Overwhelmed by DIY-kit choice; unsure where cameras should go; worried about drilling, wiring, and "doing it wrong"; distrusts cheap gear.                      |
| **Buying triggers**   | Recent break-in nearby; new home purchase; package theft; insurance prompt; a neighbour's visible install.                                                      |
| **Objections**        | "Is professional install worth the premium over a Ring kit?"; "Will this be reliable?"; "What if something breaks?"                                             |
| **Preferred contact** | Tap-to-call for fast questions; quote form when ready to compare.                                                                                               |

### 3b. Persona — Commercial Property / Facility Manager

| Attribute             | Detail                                                                                                                                                               |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Profile**           | Manages one or more commercial sites (retail, warehouse, office, multi-residential); accountable for security, liability, and budget; buys on spec and track record. |
| **Goals**             | Documented coverage for liability and incident review; commercial-grade hardware; a vendor who handles design, site survey, install, and ongoing support.            |
| **Pains**             | Multi-camera projects are hard to scope; needs a site survey before committing; prior installers vanished post-install; compliance and retention requirements.       |
| **Buying triggers**   | New lease/fit-out; insurance or compliance requirement; an incident on-site; expansion to a new location; aging analog system needing retrofit.                      |
| **Objections**        | "Can they handle a multi-site commercial job, not just homes?"; "Is there a warranty and real support line?"; "Will the design actually cover our blind spots?"      |
| **Preferred contact** | Quote form with project detail; expects a call-back and a scheduled site survey.                                                                                     |

### 3c. Persona (secondary) — Small-Business Owner

| Attribute             | Detail                                                                                                                                         |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Profile**           | Owner-operator of a storefront, clinic, or small office; wears every hat; budget-conscious but values being protected and present.             |
| **Goals**             | Deter theft, resolve disputes with footage, check in remotely after hours; a system that fits a small footprint without enterprise complexity. |
| **Pains**             | No IT staff; can't afford downtime or a botched install; unsure what "commercial-grade" should cost.                                           |
| **Buying triggers**   | Shrinkage/theft; a chargeback or dispute needing footage; opening a second location.                                                           |
| **Objections**        | "Is this overkill or right-sized for one shop?"; "How fast can it be installed?"                                                               |
| **Preferred contact** | Tap-to-call, then quote form.                                                                                                                  |

## 4. Value Proposition & Positioning

**Positioning statement:** For commercial and residential property owners who want security that actually works, Soo Solutions Inc supplies and professionally installs commercial-grade camera systems — certified, warrantied, and locally supported — so you get the protection of pro-grade hardware without the gamble of doing it yourself.

**Supporting proof:**

- **Certified technicians** install the system — not a homeowner with a drill.
- **Warranty assured** — the work and the hardware are backed.
- **Official partner brands** — Lorex, Hikvision, HiLook — i.e. commercial-grade supply, not consumer kits.
- **Tailored to both segments** — residential and commercial solutions designed per site, including system design and site survey.

## 6. Competitive Context

The site competes on two fronts. The counter-position on both is the same: professional installation, warranty, local presence, and commercial-grade partner brands — a combination neither the local generalist nor the national DIY brand offers together.

| Competitor type                                                    | Their pitch                                          | Our counter-position                                                                                  | Our differentiation                                                                                                                                                               |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Local independent installers / electricians**                    | "I'm local and I can wire it up."                    | We are a security specialist, not a generalist who also does cameras.                                 | Specialist focus; certified techs; named partner brands; warranty assured; reliable support line; commercial + residential design and site survey.                                |
| **National DIY / self-monitor kits** (Ring, Wyze, Nest, ADT-style) | "Cheap, ship-it-yourself, monitor it on your phone." | Pro-grade hardware, professionally installed and warrantied, with a local team who surveys your site. | Professional install (no DIY gamble); commercial-grade Lorex/Hikvision/HiLook supply; local site survey and support; warranty on work and hardware; tailored per-property design. |

## 8. Scope

### In scope (MVP)

- The marketing website: Home, About, Services hub + detail, Industries, Solutions, Work/Case Studies hub, Products, Service Areas hub + city pages, FAQ, Contact/Quote. (Canonical sitemap lives in [PRD](./PRD.md) and [TRD](./TRD.md).)
- **Quote funnel** — contact/quote form plus tap-to-call as the primary conversion path.
- **Local SEO** — per-city service-area pages, sitemap/robots, Google Business Profile alignment.
- **Partner showcase** — Lorex / Hikvision / HiLook products presented as a marketing showcase (no cart, no prices).
- Utility/system pages: `/privacy`, `/terms`, sitemap, robots, 404, OG image.

### Out of scope (MVP)

- E-commerce / checkout / cart / prices.
- Customer portal / login / accounts.
- Live 24/7 monitoring dashboard.
- Blog (deferred from MVP).
- Careers page (deferred — see Phase-2 in [PRD](./PRD.md)).
- Case-study detail pages `/work/[slug]` (Phase-2; hub ships MVP with 1–2 featured).
