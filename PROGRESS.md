## Decisions log

- **2026-06-18** — **Interactive hero camera animates by lerping in `useFrame`, not a spring lib.** Considered `@react-spring/three` but chose a dependency-free `useFrame` lerp so the swap state machine (exit → commit → enter), the shutter-flash emissive pulse, and **R3F's automatic dispose-on-unmount** (one model mounted at a time) all stay under direct control with nothing new in the 3D chunk. Camera types are R3F primitives (Bullet/Dome/Turret/PTZ/Doorbell) sharing brand materials; names/order come from `cameraTypes.ts`. Reduced-motion swaps the per-type product photo instantly (no WebGL, per `soo-motion-3d`); mobile keeps the static poster.
- **2026-06-18** — **Filtered one third-party deprecation to reach a 100% clean console.** `@react-three/fiber` 9.6.1 constructs `THREE.Clock`, which `three` 0.184 deprecates for `THREE.Timer` — the only console output the hero scene produced (the dev-overlay "1 Issue"). `silence-three-warnings.ts` drops exactly that message (nothing else) until R3F ships a Timer loop.
